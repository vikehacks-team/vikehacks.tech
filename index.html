VikeHacks is a 12 hour hackathon, where high school students that participate have an opportunity to create a lasting impact in their community. It is a 12 hour event where students of all skill levels in programming come to solve real world problems through the use of coding. A hackathon includes workshops to help students get started on their projects as well as plently of working time to work on and develop projects.
