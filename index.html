What is VikeHacks?

VikeHacks is a 12 hour hackathon where students of all skill levels in programming come to solve real world problems through the use of coding. VikeHacks includes workshops to help students get started on their projects as well as plenty of working time to work on and develop projects.

What if I don't know how to code?

No worries! VikeHacks is the perfect time to learn how to code and all high school students, regardless of skill level and experience, are welcome. There will be plenty of mentors on hand glad to answer any and all questions and workshops specifically designed for those with no prior experience. Stay tuned for more information about workshops! College students are welcome to help out as mentors!

What if I don't have a team or an idea?

No worries! It is not uncommon for people to come into the hackathon without an already-formed group, but we'll make sure you're matched with the right people! We will find a team formation and ideation session the first night so everyone can find the perfect team and come up with awesome ideas, so don't worry about first-time hackers! Teams can be up to four people. While you may work as an individual, we highly encourage collaborating with others. If you don't have a team or need help finding one, make sure not to miss our team formation and ideation session.

What Should I Bring?

Attendees should bring a valid student ID or some other form of identification, as well as a signed and completed waiver. Bring laptops and chargers, along with any other technology that you may want to use! We will have designated sleeping spots so feel free to bring a sleeping bag and pillow. Also, it is highly suggested that you bring any toiletries you would like to use. This event is completely free, so just bring you and your ideas. We will provide you with the food, WiFi, workspace, and swag!
